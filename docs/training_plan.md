# Capacity Building and Training Plan

## Audience
- Regulator staff
- Distribution company KPI teams
- SCADA / DMS engineers
- Billing and metering teams
- Management reporting teams

## Training Modules

### Module 1 — KPI Concepts
- SAIDI, SAIFI, CAIDI
- Losses: technical vs non-technical
- Collection efficiency
- Data quality

### Module 2 — Data Requirements
- Mandatory fields
- Source systems
- Monthly reporting templates
- Ownership of each data field

### Module 3 — Data Validation
- Missing values
- duplicate events
- unrealistic outage duration
- customer count consistency
- energy balance checks

### Module 4 — Dashboard Interpretation
- executive KPI cards
- monthly trends
- benchmarking
- management flags

### Module 5 — Regulatory Use
- incentive mechanisms
- performance improvement plans
- auditability and transparency
